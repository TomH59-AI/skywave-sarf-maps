Questions

What exactly is a SCIP?
SCIP stands for Site Candidate Identification Package. It includes setting your SARF anchor coordinates, auto-scoring all parcels within the 0.5-mile ring, selecting optimal targets A through E, and generating professional mailer letters to each property owner.
What do I get for free?
The complete mailer generation workflow. Set your site, see the auto-picked targets, open the mailer, customize your letter with your company name and lease terms, and preview the full professional output. No credit card required.
What does $49 unlock?
The ability to export and print all your mailer letters, pick Target D and E, access full zoning ordinance details, and download the CSV of all targets. One payment = one complete SCIP finished and ready to mail.
Is my payment secure?
All payments are processed by Stripe, the same payment infrastructure used by Amazon, Google, and millions of businesses. We never see or store your card number.
